          What is the ellipse icon?
          The ellipsis ... (also known informally as dot dot dot) is a series of dots that indicates an intentional omission of a word, sentence, or whole section from a text without altering its original meaning. The plural is ellipses. The term originates from the Ancient Greek: , lleipsis meaning 'leave out'.
